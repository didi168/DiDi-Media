About Me

Front-End Developer & Creative Designer

I'm a skilled and innovative Front-End Developer and Creative Designer with a passion for crafting seamless user experiences and visually compelling content. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Next.js, and TypeScript, combined with professional skills in graphic design, video editing, and motion graphics, I bring a unique blend of technical and creative expertise to every project.

My experience spans developing responsive web applications, creating brand identities, editing promotional videos, and producing 2D animations for marketing campaigns. I've worked with brands like Apex Boost Socials and HostCreep, delivering engaging visual content using tools like Figma, Adobe Premiere Pro, CapCut Pro, and Jitter Video.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Biology from the University of Port Harcourt and have volunteered as both a Computer Science Teacher and Graphics & Video Editing Instructor, sharing my passion for technology and creativity with aspiring learners.
